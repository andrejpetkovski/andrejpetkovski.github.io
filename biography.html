Some of the Projects I have worked on

Online Quiz App

The Online Quiz App is an interactive platform designed to create, manage, and take quizzes online. This app allows users to register, create custom quizzes, and participate in quizzes created by others. It features real-time scoring, user profiles, and a leaderboard to encourage competition. The app aims to provide an engaging and educational experience for users of all ages.
Technologies used in the development of this app include: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Express.js.

Tic-Tac-Toe

The Tic-Tac-Toe game is a classic two-player strategy game implemented as a digital application. Players take turns marking Xs and Os on a 3x3 grid, aiming to place three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row. The game offers a simple yet engaging way to challenge friends or the computer with different difficulty levels.
Technologies used in the development of this app include: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Express.js.
